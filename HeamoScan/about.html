Meet Our Team

We Are HemoScan

We are a passionate team of 5 second-year B.Tech students from Computer Science - AIML at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. Our enthusiasm for innovation and technology drives us to create impactful solutions like HemoScan.

With a strong foundation in AI, machine learning, and healthcare applications, we aim to make hemoglobin monitoring more accessible and user-friendly. Our dedication to problem-solving and teamwork helps us push the boundaries of AI-driven healthcare.

Join us on our journey as we strive to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare!
