How to Scan Your Nails for HB Calculation

1. Live Scanning of Nails

Follow these steps for an accurate HB reading:

  • ✅ Position the camera so **only your nails** are visible.
  • ✅ Ensure **no objects or people** are near you.
  • ✅ Maintain a **proper distance** for a clear scan.
  • ✅ Use a **clean, undamaged camera** for accuracy.

2. Upload an Existing Image

Ensure your image meets these requirements:

  • ✅ The image should be **clear and zoomed in** so nail color is visible.
  • ✅ **Allowed formats:** JPG & PNG only.
